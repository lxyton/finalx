Spotlight Vehicle of the Day

2013 BMW 335i Metallic Black

This 1:5 BMW is on listing by owner Frank Ocean. He is widely known for his collection of foreign vehicles (mostly bmw's). Customized by Frank himself, this 335i consists of a twin turbo, 6-cylinder engine and 600 HP. Toyo Proxes 2888 racing tires, averaging at around $10,000 per tire. Mr.Ocean even contacted the infamous Leyo Zigarillo to hand-craft the interior with perfection coated over the whole exterior, metallic black paint.

Price: $2,000,000
